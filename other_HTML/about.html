Exotic Essence- A TRUSTED, HOUSEHOLD NAME

ExoticEssence (Pvt) Ltd. was formed in the year 2000, by the Rajapaksha family as a spice and herb manufacturing company that markets the “ExoticEssence” brand. Today “ExoticEssence” is a popular choice among Sri Lankan households and a much sought after brand in all parts of the island when it comes to authentic, high quality spices and herbs

MANUFACTURER OF HIGH QUALITY SPICES AND TRADITIONAL SRI LANKAN CURRIES

With a plethora of spices products, the Exotic Essence range brings you pure, unadulterated, high quality spices and herbs that can turn any dish into a delicacy. Whole spices, spice blends, curries,. ready-to-eat jar products such as chutneys, baduns(fries), pastes, sauces, pickles and sambols are among the variety of ExoticEssence products we are planning to introduce. Our newest addition is the Masala range of pre-cooked curry sauces which is a popular product among local and expatriate clientele.

Sri Lankan households, leading supermarkets, hotels and restaurants, food manufacturers and expatriates are those who build up our clientele; who trust and vouch for the superior quality of the “ExoticEssence” range of products.
